**GitHub:** bludragon66613-sys
**Active design projects:** drip (peptide creator platform), Munshi (AI accounting), NERV dashboard, Morning Light Energy

**Taste tier:** Linear / Stripe / Vercel / Apple. Japanese minimalism. Billion-dollar product quality. No tacky effects.

**How he signals bad design:** "absolute garbage", "slop", "trash". If any of these appear in session feedback, the design missed the brand bible and/or used generic dark palette without reading the project's actual tokens.

**How he signals good design:** Accepts the output without critique. In drip's case, the Swiss Clinical direction (paper/ink/red-accent) with typography as primary visual system is the locked direction.

**Key preferences:**
- Typography first. No decorative effects.
- Brand marks always included on public surfaces.
- No em-dash, no double-hyphen, no section mark in copy.
- Lowercase brand names where the bible says so (drip always lowercase).
- Agents and humans treated as peer customers in drip copy.
- Data shown through numbers and mono type, not charts or icons.
