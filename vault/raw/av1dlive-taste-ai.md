## Summary
The core thesis: AI made execution free but also made average the new mediocre. Taste is the new core skill.

## Key Points
1. **Statistical taste vs curated taste** — AI defaults to cultural average (safe-good)
2. **The gap = your taste** — space between AI output and what you actually want
3. **Generate, then destroy** — produce 10 versions, build rejection vocabulary
4. **AI can't replace taste with perspective** — taste backed by lived experience
5. **75-25 rule** — AI gets you to 75th percentile easily. The delta in next 25% is pure taste
6. **Specificity test** — if output could be swapped to any product unchanged, it has no taste

## Applied to Munshi
- Every Munshi output must pass the specificity test for Indian SME accounting
- Competitor research, UX design, GTM strategy — all curated, not safe-good
- Paperclip agents built with taste guardrails
