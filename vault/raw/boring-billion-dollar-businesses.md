## Summary
10 sectors positioned for billion-dollar outcomes by 2027. The pattern: boring, domain-specific, deeply embedded software in regulation-heavy industries.

## Munshi-Relevant: AI-Powered Accounting (Sector #9)
- Market size: 4.9-6.6B today -> 29-97B by 2030 at 39.6% CAGR
- 75% of CPAs retiring in next decade = massive vacuum
- 60+ AI-focused accounting/tax startups raised capital 2023-2025
- 82% of early adopters reported positive ROI in year one
- 83% of accounting professionals now use some form of AI
- Revenue model: 200-2000/month per business, recurring
- Key insight: nobody wants to be an accounting startup, which means no Big Tech competition

## The Common Entry Strategy
1. Start hyper-niche - own one use case in one industry
2. Use AI APIs, don't train models - moat is domain data
3. Find 3-5 design partners willing to pay early
4. Build for outcome-based pricing
5. Data as your moat - every transaction makes system smarter

## Critical Anti-Patterns
- Selling AI tools is vulnerable. Selling AI-powered outcomes is defensible.
- LLM wrapper companies face extinction
- 95% of orgs see no business return from gen AI despite billions in spending
- Revenue gap: 400B infrastructure investment vs 100B revenue (4:1 ratio)

## The Point
"The boring businesses aren't just the safest bet in AI. They might be the only bet that works."
