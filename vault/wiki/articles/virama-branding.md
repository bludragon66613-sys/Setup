Virāma (विराम) is SSquare's first residential project — 12 private villas in Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad. The branding project covers the developer brand (SSquare) and the residential project brand (Virāma), plus a standalone 10-brand pipeline.

**Repo:** `github.com/bludragon66613-sys/branding-pipeline`
**Files:** `branding-pipeline/projects/virama/`

## Key Brand Facts

- **Project:** Virāma — 12 private villas, Rs 13–20 Cr per unit
- **Location:** Prashaanjan Nagar, Jubilee Hills (branded as "Malibu")
- **Developer:** SSquare — est. 1991, 35 years, 10M+ sqft commercial real estate
- **Developer tagline:** "The Standard, Privately Held."
- **Project tagline:** "Come Home to Quiet."
- **Logomark:** S² monogram
- **Brand is generic to SSquare** — no founders named, company-level brand
