The `steipete/summarize` tool provides fast summaries from a wide array of sources, including URLs, local files, and various media types, available through a command-line interface, Chrome Side Panel, or Firefox Sidebar. It handles diverse inputs such as web pages, PDFs, images, audio/video, YouTube, podcasts, and RSS feeds. A key feature is its *'Slide extraction for video sources (YouTube/direct media) with OCR + timestamped cards'*, which helps users quickly grasp video content. The tool also offers *'Streaming output with Markdown rendering, metrics, and cache‑aware status'* and integrates with local, paid, and free AI models, including OpenAI-compatible endpoints and an OpenRouter free preset, for flexible transcription and summarization.
