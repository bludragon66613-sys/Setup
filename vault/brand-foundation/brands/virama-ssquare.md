# Virāma by SSquare

Luxury residential branding project. SSquare's first residential venture after 35 years in commercial real estate.

## Project facts

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Project** | Virāma (विराम) |
| **Type** | 12 private villas, Rs 13–20 Cr |
| **Location** | Prashashan Nagar, Jubilee Hills (branded "Malibu") |
| **Developer** | SSquare (est. 1991, 35 years, 10M+ sqft commercial RE) |
| **Developer tagline** | "The Standard, Privately Held." |
| **Project tagline** | "Come Home to Quiet." |
| **Logomark** | S² monogram |

## Brand tone

Serene opulence. **Minimal in expression, maximal in experience.** Hushed confidence. Never loud. Never aspirational-striving. The brand speaks as someone who has already arrived.
