# Munshi

AI accounting intelligence for Indian SMEs. Brand bible v3.0 approved April 5, 2026.

## Identity

- **Direction:** A — Stripe Neelam (surgical precision, global fintech aesthetic)
- **Category:** AI accounting intelligence
- **Repo:** github.com/bludragon66613-sys/Munshi (renamed from tallyai)
- **Authoritative files:**
  - `~/tallyai/brand/MUNSHI_BRAND_BIBLE.html` (2,987 lines, interactive)
  - `~/tallyai/brand/MUNSHI_BRAND_BIBLE.pdf` (1.9 MB, print-ready)
  - `~/tallyai/brand/concepts/direction-a-stripe-neelam.html`

## Hard rules

1. **NO Devanagari script.** Ever. All Hindi is in Roman/English alphabet only.
2. **Use brand bible tokens exactly.** Never improvise. If a token is not in the bible, ask.
3. **No glow, no gradients on surfaces, no stock photos, no pills.**
4. **Logo is sacred.** Always include. Never redraw.

## Logo

- Open-book monogram with Neelam Green gradient `#1B6B4A → #2A9D8F`
- Wordmark: `Munshi.` in DM Sans 700
- Gold dot (`#E8A317`) on the period — this is the premium/AI marker

## Spacing + structure

- **Grid:** 4px base (4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 80)
- **Page padding:** 80px
- **Border radius:** sm 4px, md 8px, lg 12px, xl 16px, full 9999px (pills only on badges)
- **Cards:** white bg, 1px solid `#DFE2E8`, radius 12px, shadow `0 1px 3px rgba(27,107,74,.08)`, hover: `translateY(-1px)` + `shadow-raised`
- **Hover transition:** 0.15s ease default
- **3px gradient accent line:** top of page, `linear-gradient(135deg, #1B6B4A, #2A9D8F)`

## References

Stripe. Linear. Notion. Vercel. Apple. Japanese aesthetic principles: **Ma** (negative space), **Kanso** (simplicity), **Shibui** (understated elegance).

## Do not

- Devanagari script anywhere
- Pills on buttons (only badges)
- Approximate/generic SaaS styling
- Shadows that aren't Neelam-blue-tinted
- Kesar Gold on anything that isn't AI-related, the logo dot, or a premium accent
- Effects without purpose (see [[../design-antipatterns]])
