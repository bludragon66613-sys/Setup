Design concepts must integrate existing brand marks (e.g., BookOpen monogram for Munshi). Gradient-heavy, glow-heavy, over-decorated designs read as "tacky." 

**Why:** Rohan flagged concepts D/E/F as tacky — too many effects, missing the logo, not clean enough. Prefers Japanese design principles and billion-dollar product aesthetics.

**How to apply:** When building design concepts, reference Stripe, Linear, Notion, Vercel, Apple. Apply Ma (negative space), Kanso (simplicity), Shibui (understated elegance). Less is more. Every element must earn its place. Always include existing brand marks. No gratuitous gradients or glow effects.
