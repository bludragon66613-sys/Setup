# AI Distribution Strategies (2026)

**Source:** Greg Isenberg / The Startup Ideas Podcast  
**Date:** 2026-03-31  
**Link:** https://x.com/startupideaspod/status/2038697353855787133

## Core Thesis

"Everyone's vibe coding. Almost no one's getting customers." Code is commoditized. Distribution is the new moat. The builders who understand how to get customers, build audiences, and earn attention are at the top of the stack.

## The 7 Strategies

### 1. MCP Servers as Your Sales Team
Users ask AI assistants a question. AI discovers your MCP server. Your product is returned as the answer.
- **Zero CAC.** The AI assistant becomes your 24/7 sales team.
- **Action:** Build an MCP server that returns your core data. Publish it to registries like Smithery, MCPT, and OpenTools.

### 2. Programmatic SEO
Pick a keyword pattern (e.g., "best CRMs for dentists"). Get structured data. Build a page template. Use AI to generate unique content per page.
- **The Math:** 10,000 pages × 30 visits/mo × 2% conversion × $10 = $60,000/month.
- **Action:** Use tools like Firecrawl to scrape clean data. Publish 100 pages as your MVP.

### 3. Free Tool as Top of Funnel
A free tool (like Ahrefs' backlink checker) acts as the hook. User gets value, shares their result, new users discover the tool, you upsell.
- **Action:** Ship a free tool in a day. It markets itself forever.

### 4. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
SEO gets you on Google page one. AEO gets you cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity. Write structured, direct, citation-worthy answers.
- **Action:** Google the top 20 questions your customer asks. Add schema markup and FAQ blocks.

### 5. Viral Artifacts
Spotify Wrapped, GitHub contribution graphs. Find the output or milestone your user would screenshot. Make it beautiful, shareable, and subtly branded.
- **Action:** Add a share button that pre-fills the post. Let your users do your marketing.

### 6. Buy a Newsletter
Building an audience takes years. Buying a 10,000-subscriber newsletter for $5K-$20K is a shortcut to immediate trust.
- **Action:** Browse Duuce.com or Newsletter Investor. DM owners in your niche.

### 7. AI Content Repurposing Engine
One pillar piece of content (30-min voice memo) becomes 5-10 tweets, LinkedIn posts, short videos, and a newsletter.
- **Action:** Transcribe your content. Feed it into an LLM with specific format instructions. Optimize for your voice.

## Application for TallyAI (Munshi)
- **MCP:** A TallyAI MCP server for answering "how do I analyze this Tally XML?"
- **Programmatic SEO:** "Tally analytics for [specific Indian industry]" pages.
- **Free Tool:** A free Tally XML file analyzer/validator.
- **AEO:** Detailed, structured answers to "best Tally reporting tools in India."
